Delete Account Instructions

Please follow the steps below to delete your account from the app:

Step 1: Open the App

Launch the app on your device.

Step 2: Go to Account Settings

Tap on the profile icon or navigate to the settings menu to access your account settings.

Step 3: Find Delete Account Option

Scroll down to find the Delete Account option.

Step 4: Confirm Deletion

Tap on Delete Account and follow the prompts to confirm the deletion.

Step 5: Enter Credentials

Enter your account password or other required credentials to complete the process.

After completing these steps, your account will be deleted from the app.
